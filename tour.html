How It Works

Find, book, and receive services in just a few simple steps

For Customers

1

Search for a Service

Browse our extensive directory of verified service providers or use our search feature to find the specific service you need in your area.

Tips:
  • Use specific keywords for better results
  • Filter by location, rating, and price range
  • Check provider profiles for specializations
2

Book an Appointment

Select an available time slot that works for you and book your appointment with just a few clicks. Our streamlined booking process makes scheduling services easy and convenient.

What you'll need:
  • Your preferred date and time
  • Service location details
  • Brief description of the service needed
3

Receive & Rate Service

After your service is completed, you can rate and review your provider, helping others make informed decisions while building a trustworthy community.

Remember to:
  • Provide honest, constructive feedback
  • Rate your overall experience
  • Add specific comments about the service

Frequently Asked Questions

All service providers on our platform undergo a verification process that includes identity verification, credential checks, and reference verification. Additionally, you can view ratings and reviews from previous customers to make informed decisions.

You can cancel your booking through your account dashboard. Our cancellation policy depends on how much notice you provide. Cancellations made more than 24 hours in advance typically receive a full refund, while last-minute cancellations may incur a small fee to compensate the service provider for their time.

Service providers set their own prices based on their experience, qualifications, and market rates. Servzo does not control pricing, but we do provide transparency by showing all costs upfront before you book. Some providers offer fixed rates while others may provide estimates that can be adjusted based on the actual work performed.

Basic registration as a service provider is free. We operate on a commission-based model, taking a small percentage of each successfully completed booking. Premium features and enhanced visibility options are available for providers looking to grow their business faster.

We offer secure payment processing through our platform. Customers can pay using credit/debit cards or other supported payment methods. For most services, payment is only released to the service provider after the service has been completed and you confirm your satisfaction, providing an extra layer of protection for customers.
